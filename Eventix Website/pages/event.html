Important Guidelines

Profit Pursuit is not just a race—its a battle of wits and strategic thinking. Participants will navigate through multiple rounds designed to simulate real-world business challenges. From answering commerce-based questions to managing virtual funds and completing tasks across campus locations, teams will be required to make quick yet calculated decisions to maximize their profits and secure victory. The event kicks off with an online elimination round hosted on Unstop, where teams will face a timed quiz covering a wide range of topics, including: Accountancy Fundamental concepts and principles. Finance Investment strategies and financial decision-making. Marketing Market analysis, consumer behavior, and brand strategy. Business Concepts Core ideas that shape the commercial world. Only the top 10 teams with the best scores will advance to the on-campus treasure hunt, where the real adventure begins. On-Campus Rounds: The on-campus rounds consist of three exciting stages, where teams will: Answer Rapid-Fire Commerce Questions: Teams must answer a set of questions, with the fastest correct response earning the first chance to choose a chit that reveals their next task location. Navigate Across Campus to Complete Tasks: Teams will travel to various campus locations and complete commerce-related challenges. Search and Collect Products: After completing each task, teams will receive another chit with the name of a product that they must find and bring back to the venue. Each team will be given a virtual account with ₹50,000, which they must manage carefully throughout the rounds.
